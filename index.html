Our Mission

The EEO office is a resource for all community members to help eliminate harassing behavior, prevent its recurrence, and address its effects. Supportive, safe and fair responses to such concerns are at the heart of ensuring a shared professional work environment. All concerns are taken seriously, and many options are available to address such concerns. This office is committed to maintaining a Lab environment free from harassment and other forms of discrimination.
